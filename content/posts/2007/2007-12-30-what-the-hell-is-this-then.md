"What the hell is this, then?"
  
"f I didn't know better
  
I'd say it was poetry."

Well, that's exactly what it is, I say.
  
Especially since you don't know better
  
anyway.
  
It may not look like poetry
  
unless, of course
  
I wrap each
  
line
  
like this.

Then who's to say?
