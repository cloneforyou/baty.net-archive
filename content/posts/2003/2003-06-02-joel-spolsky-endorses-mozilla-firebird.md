day-to-day browsing any more.
  


> "All the little problems are fixed. It loads fast. It's not ugly and
  
> 
  
> clunky. My beloved Alt+D/Ctrl+Enter work perfectly. NT
  
>

>

>

> 
  
> Excellent cookie management."

Amen to that.
